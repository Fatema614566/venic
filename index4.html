Beauty begins the moment you decide to be yourself

Beauty is a concept that has been explored and defined in countless ways throughout history.
From ancient philosophers to modern poets.
One common theme that emerges in discussions about beauty is the idea that true beauty comes from within oneself.

It is important to remember that beauty is not a fixed or static concept.
It is constantly evolving and changing, just as we are.
As we grow and learn, our understanding of beauty deepens and expands.
We come to see that true beauty is not something that can be bought or sold, but something that is cultivated and nurtured over time.
