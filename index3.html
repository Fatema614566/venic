Taking about the exam,in the writing section (section -B of the exam),it comes as a long answer type question (marks 5).Generally, there is a choice (internal) between the folowing:

  • Article writing
  • Report writing

The question for report is supposed to be answered within the prescribed limit of 120-50 words.

Talking about marks, its comes as a 5 marks questions. The breakup for which (as per CBSE marking scheme) is as follows:

Formate(Heading & Reporters name) 01 marks Organization of ideas 01 marks Content 01 marks Spelling and grammar 01 marks
